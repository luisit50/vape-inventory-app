Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Vape Inventory ("we", "our", or "us") collects the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address and password for authentication
  • Inventory Data: Information about bottles you add to your inventory (images, dates, notes)
  • Camera Access: We use your camera to capture bottle images (images are stored on our servers)
  • Purchase Information: Subscription details managed through RevenueCat and Google Play

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Vape Inventory service
  • Process and store your bottle inventory data
  • Process OCR (text recognition) on bottle images
  • Manage your subscription and account
  • Improve our services and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management
  • Google Play: For in-app purchases
  • Tesseract OCR: For text recognition in images

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
